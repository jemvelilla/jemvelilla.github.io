About Rehd and Jem

Jem and Rehd first met during the Thomasian Welcome Walk and they became partners as flag bearers. It might have been God's plan that they met during their first day as College students.

After that day, they never saw each other again until they became teammates for a competition. It's really amazing to think that after their first year in the University, they became classmates.

Maybe God allowed them to know each other better. Until March 07, 2017 that Jem realized that she wanted Rehd to be part of her life.
